## 1. Information we collect

**Information you give us**

- **Account and contact details** — such as your name, email address, phone
  number, and delivery or project address.
- **Photos of your space** — images you upload or capture to preview Amira
  finishes in your room using the Interior Vision Studio.
- **Messages and conversations** — what you send to the Amira assistant and any
  follow-up conversation with a member of the Amira team.
- **Requests, quotes, and orders** — the products, finishes, and details you
  select, along with notes you share about your project.
- **Appointments** — the consultations or visits you request and related notes.
- **Preferences** — items you save or favourite, and settings you choose.

**Information we collect as you use the Services**

- **Activity information** — for example, the designs and products you view and
  the features you use, so we can understand what's helpful and improve the
  experience.
- **Basic device and app information** — general details needed to deliver the
  app reliably and keep it secure.

We only ask for what we need to provide the Services well.

## 2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Generate previews of your space and power the design and discovery features.
- Respond to your messages, including when a member of the Amira team joins a
  conversation to help you directly.
- Prepare quotes, process design requests and orders, and arrange delivery,
  installation, consultations, and appointments.
- Keep you informed with service messages, updates about your requests, and—
  where you've agreed—news and offers.
- Understand how the Services are used so we can improve them.
- Keep the Services safe, prevent misuse, and meet our legal and accounting
  obligations.

## 3. Your photos and the design features

The photos you share for the Interior Vision Studio are used to create design
previews for you and to help the Amira team understand and respond to your
project. Please avoid including other people or sensitive personal documents in
the photos you upload. If you'd like us to remove a photo you've shared, contact
us using the details below.

## 4. The Amira assistant and human follow-up

The Amira assistant helps answer questions and suggest ideas. To give you the
best help, a member of the Amira team may review a conversation and step in to
respond personally. When that happens, you may see an indication that a
specialist has joined the conversation. We keep a record of conversations so we
can follow up, improve our help, and maintain a history of your requests.

## 5. How we share information

We do **not** sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run
the Services, including with:

- **Trusted partners and suppliers** who help us deliver the Services—for
  example, delivery, installation, payment handling, communications, and
  technology support—and who are expected to protect your information and use it
  only for the services they provide to us.
- **Professional advisers** such as accountants and lawyers, where appropriate.
- **Authorities or other parties** when we reasonably believe it is required by
  law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of
  our customers, the public, or Amira.
- **A successor** in the event of a business reorganisation, merger, or sale,
  consistent with this policy.

## 6. Communications and notifications

We may send you service-related messages (for example, about your orders,
requests, or appointments) and, with your agreement where required, news and
offers. You can adjust notification preferences in the app or unsubscribe from
marketing messages at any time. Some essential service messages are needed to
provide the Services and cannot be switched off while you have an account.

## 7. Keeping your information

We keep your information for as long as you have an account and as long as needed
to provide the Services, meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, resolve
disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we
take steps to delete it or remove what identifies you.

## 8. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
- Ask us to delete your information, subject to legal limits.
- Object to or limit certain uses, and withdraw consent where we rely on it.
- Receive a copy of certain information you provided to us.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below. We may need to
confirm your identity first, and we will respond within the time required by
applicable law. You can also update many details directly in your profile.

## 9. Keeping your information secure

We use reasonable measures designed to protect your information against loss,
misuse, and unauthorised access. No service can be completely secure, so we
encourage you to use a strong password, keep your device protected, and contact
us right away if you think your account has been compromised.

## 10. Children

The Services are intended for adults and are not directed at children. We do not
knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided
us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

## 11. Where your information is handled

Amira operates in East Africa and may work with partners located elsewhere. Where
information is handled outside your country, we take steps to ensure it remains
protected consistent with this policy and applicable law.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material
changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, let you
know in the app. Please review it periodically.
