# Amira Luxury — Project Documentation

## About Amira Interiors

> "At Amira Interiors, we see luxury as more than just beautiful spaces — it's about creating an experience. The feeling a room gives, the atmosphere it creates, and the way every detail comes together all matter just as much as the design itself."

Amira Interiors redefines luxury interiors in East Africa through carefully chosen textures, wall panels, finishes, lighting, and modern design elements — creating spaces that feel refined, timeless, and personal.

---

## App Overview

An AI-powered luxury interior design app. The UI is being built first with dummy data. Backend integration will be handled by a separate team.

Target: Premium, warm, and visually immersive experience that reflects the Amira brand identity.

## Core Features

### 1. Interior Vision Studio
- User uploads a photo or takes a live image of a room
- AI analyzes the space and overlays Amira's interior products (wooden wall finishes, panels, textures, lighting, etc.)
- User can explore different product applications on their actual space
- Think of it as an AR/AI try-on but for interiors

### 2. Explore Designs
- A curated gallery of completed and concept interior designs
- Showcases Amira's design language — refined, timeless, East African luxury
- Users can browse by room type, style, material, or mood
- Each design links to the products used

### 3. Order / Request Page
- User browses Amira's catalog of interior materials and products
- Selects preferred items and combinations
- Submits a design request or order to the Amira team
- Form-based flow with a luxury feel — no clutter, intentional design

### 4. AI Chatbot (Gemini-style)
- Conversational assistant embedded in the app
- Helps users explore styles, ask about products, get design advice
- Warm, knowledgeable tone that reflects the Amira brand
